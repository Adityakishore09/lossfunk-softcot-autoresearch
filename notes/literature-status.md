# Literature status

## Confirmed primary-source finding

The official SoftCoT paper trains a projection module per task and evaluates benchmark performance after that task-specific training. Its paper and public code do not provide the exact GSM8K-trained, target-untuned ASDiv-Aug versus StrategyQA transfer result selected here. Therefore, this spike is a proposed diagnostic extension, not a reproduction of a reported cross-task transfer table.

## What remains to verify before any novelty claim

The submitted proposal correctly notes existing soft-prompt transfer work, including SPoT and prompt-transferability studies. This spike must not claim that cross-task continuous-prompt transfer is new. The narrower question is whether the *relative* transfer of a source-trained SoftCoT component tracks the selected related-versus-unrelated task contrast under matched controls.

A broader literature review is still required before drafting. Current web-search access failed, so no claim about exhaustive novelty has been made.
